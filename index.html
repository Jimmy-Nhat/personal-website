About me

PERSONALLY,

I am the type of person who loves creativity and perfection, which are also expressed in my paintings. It seems that I can not only receive relaxing moments from drawing, but also become more careful, diligent, and responsible for whatever I do. To generate masterpieces without any mistakes, I spend a lot of time describing the pictures in detail. For me, drawing is truly the most attractive work in which artists strive to capture and then express the soul of things.

HOWEVER,

I only consider painting as a hobby, or simply a side job. Since I was a child, I was inspired by my older brother about electricity and technology. Becoming older, I was immersed in Physics, Informatics in particular, and Science in general. And that finally leads me to be on the way to becoming a developer.
